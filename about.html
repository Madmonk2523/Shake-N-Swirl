About Shake N' Swirl

All the flavors you crave — Shakes, bowls & more! Clean, friendly, and proudly serving Whitestone.

Our Story

Born from a love of fresh ingredients and classic treats, Shake N' Swirl brings the best of Brazilian acai bowls, vibrant pitaya creations, creamy old-fashioned milkshakes, refreshing smoothies, and self-serve frozen yogurt under one roof. Whether you're grabbing a quick wellness shot or building your dream bowl, we make it fresh, friendly, and fun.

Fresh & Real

We use quality bases, fruits, and toppings — made to order and tailored to your taste.

Clean & Caring

Customers rave about our clean machines, fresh toppings, and welcoming service.

Community Heart

Located in Whitestone with ample parking — we’re your neighborhood spot to sip, swirl, and enjoy.
